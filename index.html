Our Legacy & Philosophy

Crafting the Perfect Blend of Elegance & Strength

At Jeek Lights, we believe outdoor lighting is more than just visibility; it defines character, safety, and modern identity. Every path needs brightness, and every space deserves elegance. By blending time-honored cast iron aesthetics with sleek, innovative geometric designs, we build structures that look outstanding during the day and perform flawlessly at night.

From our headquarters in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, we manufacture premium outdoor solutions ranging from heavy-duty structural electric poles to architectural luminaires. At Jeek Lights, every project ends with elegance, structural strength, and your absolute satisfaction.

Our Core Differentiators

Why Municipalities & Designers Trust Jeek Lights

Superior Durability

Hot-dip galvanization and FG 200 double cast iron coating prevents rust even in tropical high-humidity coastal zones.

Bespoke Customization

Have a unique architectural vision? Our engineering team works closely to design custom brackets, heights, and molds.

Modern & Heritage

Whether you need modern clean geometric structures or 19th-century royal vintage cast columns, we cover the spectrum.

Optic Performance

Our luminaires offer high-lumens LED chips, uniform light spreading lenses, and top-tier thermal cooling management.
